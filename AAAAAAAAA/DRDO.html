DRDO

What's DRDO ?


DRDO Defense Research & Development Organization was formed in 1958 and it had only around 10 laboratories. But now, DRDO has come a long way to have more than 50 laboratories under its umbrella. If you are an engineer passionate about the subject and curious about research and development then DRDO offers you with one of a kind opportunity to serve the country. Aspiring candidates can take up the DRDO 2021 Recruitment Examination and get the opportunity to become a scientist developing products that will be helpful for the nation's defense system.

What's DRDO Exam ?


It is a National Level Entrance Examination conducted by the Defence Research and Development Organization of the Ministry of Defence for the position of Junior Research Fellows.

DRDO dedicatedly works towards enhancing self-reliance in Defence Systems and undertakes design & development leading to the production of world-class weapon systems and equipment per the expressed needs and the qualitative requirements laid down by the three services.

The following candidates are eligible to appear in DRDO 2021 examination :

Nationality: Candidate must be a citizen of India.

Age Limit: Upper Age Limit: Candidates more than 28 years of age(as of 31st May 2021) are not eligible for DRDO 2021 exam.
Age limit Relaxation:-

SC/ST candidates: Not exceeding 33 years (5 years of Relaxation). For Scientist 'B' Post
OBC (non-creamy layer): Not exceeding 31 years (3 years of Relaxation). For Scientist 'B' Post

For every other post, age limit relaxation is reserved as per the rules in vogue. Age is also relaxable for Armed Forces Personnel candidates as per rules in the vogue.

Minimum marks required to appear : DRDO 2021 Eligibility based on Qualification Candidates should have a first-class bachelor degree in engineering Or technology from a recognized university or institute or equivalent and valid GATE 2017/2018/2019 score.
